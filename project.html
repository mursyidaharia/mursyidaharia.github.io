Drink Your Water
Introducing my latest project – a super handy water tracker! Keep tabs on your daily hydration by clicking on the glasses representing 250mL each. It's as easy as a tap on your screen. Want to aim for that 2L goal? Just fill up the big glass by clicking through the 8 small glasses. Stay hydrated, click by click! Cheers to a well-hydrated you!

Technology used: HTML, CSS, JS

Catch the Insects
Get ready for some insect-catching fun in my latest game project! You're in control – choose your 'prey' from flies, mosquitoes, spiders, and roaches. Once you've made your pick, start clicking away! Every insect you catch adds to your score, but here's the twist – it's an impossible game to win! No time limit, but after 30 seconds, a message pops up to keep things interesting. How many insects can you nab before you got bored?

Technology used: HTML, CSS, JS
